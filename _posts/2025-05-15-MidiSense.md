MidiSense is a modular platform for gestural sound design developed together with <a href="https://nicolaprivato.com/">Nicola Privato</a>. The project was commissioned by the artist <a href="https://mariaarnalmusic.com/">Maria Arnal</a> for her performance "Ama" at <a href="https://sonar.es/es/actividad/maria-arnal-presents-ama">Sonar 2025</a>.
